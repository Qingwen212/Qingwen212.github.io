About me

I earned the PhD in Business Adiministration from the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, in 2024.
I am currently a visiting research scholar at Gies Business School of University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, U.S.A., with a joint appointment as a research assistant at the Data Science Research Services of Gies.

My research intersts lie at the intersection of information disclosures, textual analysis, natural language processing and large language models. I am especially dedicated to leveraging advanced AI/NLP/LLM technologies to address research problems in the fields of business and finance.
